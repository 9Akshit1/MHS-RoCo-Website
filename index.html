About Us

"Innovation is the future." Our club runs on this quote. We were created with the intention of educating, inspiring, and motivating others about Ro(botics) & Co(ding). We do this through participating in robotics and coding competitions. We also teach club members computer science concepts to kickstart their career.

Our goal is to inspire others to instigate change through giving back the the community through our projects. You can join and support us onsite and/or online! Participate in competitions or build friendly neighborhood-helping projects. We conduct meeting every monday in Room 129. Google classroom code is FF2Z80T.

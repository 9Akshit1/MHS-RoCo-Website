What We Do

The Outreach subteam is dedicated to engaging with our community and promoting our initiatives. We organize events, workshops, and activities that not only showcase our team's work but also inspire others to get involved. We also look for sponsors to fund the team and plan out the building plan timeline. Not only that, but we are responsible for advertising the team and school. We also manage the club's social media.
